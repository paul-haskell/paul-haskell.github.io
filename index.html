Professional Biography

Paul earned his Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at the University of California, Berkeley, specializing in video processing, compression, and transmission. After university, Paul worked at several leading-edge companies that developed video products for the entertainment television industry.

Paul currently is a Term Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of San Francisco, where he teaches several undergraduate courses.

Other Trivia

Paul was born in Berkeley, CA but he grew up on the Jersey Shore where he attended Monmouth Regional High School and worked summers at the beach. He maintains that the donuts at Delicious Orchards in Colts Neck, NJ are the world's best.

Paul has climbed several 14,000' peaks, including Mt. Rainier, Mt. Shasta, and Mt. Whitney. He enjoys backpacking, skiing, and road and mountain biking. Paul has broken 5 bones, causing friends and family to debate whether he is too adventurous, unlucky, or clumsy.
