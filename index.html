Stories

Paul earned his Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at the University of California, Berkeley, specializing in video processing, compression, and transmission. After university, Paul worked at several leading-edge companies that developed video products for the entertainment television industry.

Paul currently is a Term Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of San Francisco, where he teaches several undergraduate courses.
